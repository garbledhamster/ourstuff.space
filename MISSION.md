# Mission

## Topic

Project literacy inside the Ourstuff dashboard.

## Why This Matters

The dashboard should help a tired or distracted user understand what each area means, why status signals matter, and which small next action teaches the system. It should not be only a wall of counts, charts, and labels.

## Teaching Promise

Ourstuff teaches the user to read their own work:

- what each area is for
- where activity comes from
- why a warning or empty state matters
- what to do next without feeling judged

## Success Criteria

- The concise dashboard stays fast to scan.
- Teaching mode adds short explainers only where they help a decision.
- Every category can answer: what am I seeing, why does it matter, what should I do next.
- Warnings use plain reasons and concrete actions.
- Acronyms are expanded before they are used in first-screen copy.
